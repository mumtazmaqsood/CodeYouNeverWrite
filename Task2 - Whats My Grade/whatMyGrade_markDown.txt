# Task 2 — What’s My Grade, Really

## Overview

### The Idea
**"Encode the one set of grading rules no app has — your teacher's — and find out where you really stand."**

Most school grade apps cannot account for your instructor's specific grading policies, such as dropped lowest scores, weighted categories, conditional score replacements, or bonus rules. In this task, you will encode your teacher's exact grading system into a script that accurately calculates your current course grade.

---

## Requirements

Before starting, gather the following information:

- A complete list of your actual scores (assignments, quizzes, midterms, exams, etc.).
- Your teacher's official grading policy, including:
  - Category weights
  - Dropped score rules
  - Score replacement policies
  - Bonus or extra credit rules
- A clear understanding of how these grading rules interact.

---

### Rule Interaction

The grading system follows these steps:

1. Remove the lowest quiz score.
2. If eligible, remove the lowest assignment score.
3. Calculate averages for each category.
4. Replace the lowest Midterm score with the Final Exam score if the Final Exam is higher.
5. Apply the category weights to compute the overall course grade.
6. Add any earned bonus points (up to 3%) without allowing the final grade to exceed 100%.

---

## Steps

### 1. Collect Your Data
Compile all of your course scores along with your instructor's grading policy.

### 2. Generate the Grade Calculator
Paste your scores and grading rules into an AI assistant as plain text and ask it to generate a script that calculates your current grade.

### 3. Verify the Results
Manually calculate one grading category (such as quizzes or assignments) and compare it with the script's output to ensure the calculations are correct.

### 4. Set a Target Grade
Use the script to determine the exact score you need on the final exam to achieve your desired overall course grade.

### 5. Reuse the Script
Save the script so you can update it with new scores throughout the semester and continuously monitor your standing.

---

## Expected Outcome

By completing this task, you will have:

- An accurate calculation of your current course grade based on your instructor's actual grading policy.
- A reusable grade calculator that can be updated as new grades are released.
- A clear understanding of the minimum final exam score required to achieve your target course grade.
