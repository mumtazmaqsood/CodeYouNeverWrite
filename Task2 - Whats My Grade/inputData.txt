### Teacher's Grading Policy

The course grade is calculated using the following category weights:

| Category | Weight |
|----------|--------:|
| Assignments | 35% |
| Quizzes | 20% |
| Midterm Exams | 25% |
| Final Exam | 20% |

#### Grading Rules

- The **lowest quiz score** is automatically dropped before calculating the quiz average.
- The **lowest assignment score** is dropped if the assignment average is **85% or higher** after the drop.
- If the **Final Exam score is higher than the lowest Midterm score**, it replaces that lowest Midterm score when calculating the midterm average.
- Students can earn up to **3 bonus percentage points** toward their final course grade by completing extra-credit activities.
